# Storey European Boxing Sparring Camp

The camp takes place once a year, usually in July.

It lasts for 5 days,  from Monday to Friday night. All boxers are registered via Google forms and a weigh in happens on the Monday.


# The Venue


## Rings 
There are five rings; four are 6 foot by 6 foot and a fifth one whihc i smaller and only used by females and juniors
Every morning, the spars and ring allocation must be created. If a boxer is unfit for that day then they will be marked as unfit. 

# End Goal 
The end goal is to have the rings filled by sparring boxers.
