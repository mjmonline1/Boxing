How Boxers Are Paired

Step-by-step

  1. All registered boxers enter the system as a single group.
  2. Any boxers marked not fit will be excluded.
  3. Boxers are first separated by gender into male and female divisions.
  4. Each division is then split by age: Junior (<15), Youth (16/17), and Senior (18+).
  5. Boxers in the same category can now be paired fairly for bouts.
